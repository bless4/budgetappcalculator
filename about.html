About Us

Foresight LAB is an experience web development services company based in Malmo, Sweden.
Founded in 2017, we have successfully completed 86 projects in our portfolio with clients based across the globe.
We can work to improved your existing projects or take your business idea and bring it to live. Our goal is to give our clients a differnt taste of experience as they visit your website.

ForeSight LAb is a member of start-up Malmo, our office is located behind the malmo
central train station adjacent to the Nordea bank.
ForeSight LAB is made up of young and dynamic team of 36 workers from 26 nationality.
Divesity is our driving force. When it comes to gender we do not take it lightly. We have about 45% of women in our work force!
